Who am I? Read on to find out!

Whilst working in finance for a major sports retailer Canterbury Of New Zealand I studied Internet Solutions Development with the National IT Learning centre and became obsessed with all things internet. Since 2004 I've worked with some major companies and helped them to establish their online presence by way of full Magento or wordpress integrations and management of staff, budget control and preperation plus increasing sales.

I just love to code!
